About The Brand

Design for the places between the big cities.

Established by Kim and Taylor Homuth, StyleCo Interiors was created to bring top-tier design and home staging to the often-overlooked areas outside Austin and San Antonio.

The work is polished but practical: thoughtful finishes, edited rooms, and spaces that feel personal without losing function.
