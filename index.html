About Bastrop County

A Strategic Commercial Market Near Austin

Bastrop County offers a rare combination for Central Texas commercial real estate: access to Austin, a pro-growth business climate, expanding residential demand, and meaningful land supply along transportation corridors.

For commercial buyers and developers, the county's value is not only location. It is the mix of infrastructure investment, population growth, employer movement, natural land character, and still-emerging development patterns. That combination creates opportunity for retail growth, industrial development, commercial pads, land banking, and future mixed-use projects.

  • Business-friendly atmosphere and expanding commercial opportunities.
  • Highway access serving Austin, ABIA, SH 130, and eastern Central Texas.
  • Development potential across land, industrial, retail, and service uses.
  • Market demand driven by population growth and Austin overflow.

Investors are watching advanced manufacturing, logistics, technology-adjacent operations, retail, hospitality, and service businesses tied to SpaceX, The Boring Company, X, Samsung-area growth, and Austin overflow.
