About Taylor

Taylor Homuth

Land, development, and commercial real estate guidance rooted in Bastrop County.

A Bastrop County advisor who understands land, growth, and local relationships.

Taylor Homuth brings a land-first perspective to commercial real estate in Bastrop County and Central Texas. Raised with a ranch background, she understands the practical realities behind acreage, access, water, utilities, agricultural use, ownership transitions, and the long-term decisions that come with land.

That foundation shapes how she advises investors, developers, landowners, and business owners. Taylor helps clients evaluate commercial sites, development land, ranch and transitional acreage, corridor positioning, market timing, buyer fit, and the constraints that can affect future value.

Her commercial experience is paired with local relationships and on-the-ground knowledge of Bastrop, Smithville, and the surrounding growth corridors. She works to connect clients with the context they need before they buy, sell, hold, or reposition property.

Beyond real estate, Taylor stays connected to the community through local charities, economic initiatives, and the rural lifestyle that shaped her perspective. When she is not advising clients or structuring deals, you will often find her on horseback, at a sporting event, or enjoying time on the river with family.
